The Healthcare Crisis

⏰

Administrative Overload

Medical staff spend 4+ hours daily on manual record-taking and paperwork, reducing time available for patient care during critical situations.

📄

Paper-Based Systems

Hospitals rely on paper forms that can be lost, damaged, or become illegible. No standardized digital system exists for patient data management.

🔄

Data Isolation

Patient information cannot be easily shared between hospitals or with international medical experts, limiting care coordination and specialist consultations.

📱

Limited Digital Tools

Healthcare workers lack offline-capable tools that work with limited internet connectivity and basic hardware available in crisis zones.

SHIFAA Solutions

📷

OCR Document Scanning

Advanced ML Kit text recognition instantly digitizes medical forms and patient records with intelligent data extraction, eliminating manual data entry.

Saves 3+ hours daily
🎤

Voice-to-Text Documentation

Speech recognition for progress notes with append/replace modes, enabling rapid documentation while maintaining focus on patient care.

70% faster notes
🔐

Secure Data Export

Encrypted file export with password protection enables secure sharing of patient records between healthcare providers and international consultants.

HIPAA-level security
📱

Offline-First Design

Fully functional offline capabilities with local database storage, perfect for environments with limited connectivity.

100% offline capable
🏥

Complete Patient Management

Comprehensive patient records with admission tracking, progress notes, duplicate detection, and secure patient portal access.

End-to-end solution
🔒

Biometric Authentication

Secure doctor authentication with Face ID/fingerprint and patient login verification to protect sensitive medical data.

Military-grade security

Open Source for Global Impact

🌍 Built for the Healthcare Community

SHIFAA was developed during a hackathon focused on addressing healthcare challenges in Gaza and other crisis zones. This open-source solution can be deployed anywhere healthcare workers face similar administrative burdens and infrastructure limitations.

🎯 Hackathon Challenge Addressed:

"How might we help medical teams rapidly collect patient information in clinics or shelters using offline, low-power tools — cutting down the 4+ hours a day admin staff currently spend on manual record-taking?"
